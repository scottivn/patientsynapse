The Problem
Manual Fax Processing Is Costing You More Than You Think

Your practice receives 100–175 faxes daily. Each one is manually read, classified, typed into the EMR, and filed. Here's what that actually costs:

Staff Time

At 3–5 minutes per fax, a staff member spends 5–14 hours every day on data entry alone. That's an entire FTE consumed by copy-paste work.

Data Entry Errors

Manual transcription averages a 1–3% error rate — wrong DOB, misspelled names, missed diagnosis codes. Each error creates downstream billing and scheduling problems.

DME Bottleneck

DME supply fulfillment depends on 1–2 staff members making phone calls to patients and vendors. A single absence grinds the process to a halt.

Delayed Patient Care

Referrals sit in a physical inbox until someone gets to them. Urgent referrals can wait hours before being seen — a compliance and care-quality risk.

The Solution
Fax to EMR in Under 30 Seconds

PatientSynapse reads every incoming fax automatically, classifies the document type, extracts structured data using medical AI, and prepares it for one-click approval into your EMR.
